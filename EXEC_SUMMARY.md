## 🎯 The Opportunity

**Problem:** Africa has no beautiful, social-first invitation platform. Existing solutions (Eventbrite, Tix.Africa) are ticketing-focused, clunky, and not built for African users.

**Solution:** Party Time Africa — a Partiful-style invitation platform with:
- Beautiful, customizable invites
- Social features (guest list, comments, photo albums)
- Mobile Money integration (MTN, Airtel, M-Pesa)
- WhatsApp-first sharing
- Afrocentric design themes

**Market Size:** 400M+ internet users across Uganda, Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa

---

### 1. **Proven Model (Partiful)**
- Partiful has 40K+ 5-star reviews in the US

- "Best solution to the party-invitation problem"

### 2. **Africa-Specific Advantages**

- **WhatsApp dominance** — Africa's #1 communication platform
- **Strong party culture** — Weddings, festivals, concerts are HUGE
- **No competitor** — We can own this space
