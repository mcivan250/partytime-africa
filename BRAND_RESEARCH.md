## Brand Name Analysis

### "Party Time Africa"

**✅ PROS:**
1. **Continental vision** — Not limited to Uganda, scales to Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa
2. **Memorable** — Simple, clear, fun
3. **SEO-friendly** — "Party" + "Africa" = searchable
4. **Cultural fit** — Africa has STRONG party culture (weddings, festivals, concerts)
5. **No existing competitor** — No major "Party Time Africa" brand found
6. **Clean social handles likely available** — @partytimeafrica across platforms

**⚠️ CONSIDERATIONS:**
1. **"Africa" is broad** — Need to localize per country (language, payment methods)
2. **Infrastructure varies** — Internet speeds, mobile penetration different per region
3. **Payment complexity** — Different mobile money providers (MTN Uganda ≠ MTN Nigeria)

**🎯 VERDICT:** Perfect brand for a pan-African vision. Start Uganda, expand regionally.
